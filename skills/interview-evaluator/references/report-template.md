# Report Template

Use this for stage summaries and final interview reports.

## Final Report Structure

```text
Overall Level:
<Excellent | Strong | Passable | Weak | Risky>

Summary:
<2-4 sentences on the candidate's current interview readiness. Do not predict offer probability.>

Strengths:
- <evidence-based strength>
- <evidence-based strength>

Risks:
- <specific risk or missing evidence>
- <specific risk or missing evidence>

Dimension Review:
- Technical Depth: <level> - <evidence>
- Ownership: <level> - <evidence>
- Problem Framing: <level> - <evidence>
- Communication: <level> - <evidence>
- Reflection: <level> - <evidence>

Best Answer Moment:
<quote or paraphrase one strong answer moment, if available>

Most Important Fix:
<one highest-impact improvement>

Next Practice Plan:
1. <specific drill>
2. <specific drill>
3. <specific drill>
```

## Stage Summary Structure

```text
Stage Result:
<short conclusion>

Observed Evidence:
- <what the candidate demonstrated>

Open Questions:
- <what still needs probing>

Recommended Next Question Type:
<technical depth | ownership | tradeoff | verification | behavioral reflection>
```

## Report Rules

- Use only interview transcript, uploaded material summaries, and explicit session configuration.
- Do not claim the candidate would pass or fail a real company interview.
- Do not compare to hidden company bars unless those bars were explicitly provided in the product context.
- Do not include sensitive personal data.
- If evidence is insufficient, say so directly.

## When Transcript Is Thin

If the session has too few answers for a reliable report:

```text
Overall Level:
Insufficient evidence

Summary:
There is not enough answered material to judge interview readiness. The next session should collect at least one project-depth answer, one tradeoff answer, and one verification answer.
```
